About Me

At the start of college, I initially pursued a degree in Education with a focus on Mathematics. However, my passion for computer science soon emerged, prompting me to switch my major during my sophomore year. Alongside my major in computer science, I maintained a minor in Mathematics. This shift in academic focus proved to be highly rewarding, as I excelled in both my major and minor courses, consistently earning nearly perfect straight A's.
